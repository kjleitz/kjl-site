Welcome to my temporary website!

My name is Keegan Leitz. I'm a human being who's interested in things, generally. More specifically, I'm a person with an official degree in chemistry, and an unofficial degree in being dissatisfied with where he is right now. Even more specifically, I'm a dude with a website which, ultimately, doesn't say anything, but does help him practice web design and development.

Feel free to poke around. You might encounter a bug where no links work except for the "Home", "Wikipedia", and "GitHub" ones. Good job! But before you feel good about yourself, feel bad about yourself. Because that is not a bug! It is a feature. A feature of me never actually putting links there.

Ahem.

Anyway, check out the neat top bar. Nice little javascript trigger for some CSS animations. You can see some other CSS animations if you hover over the links at the bottom of the page. If you view this site on your phone, or if you resize your window to a narrow width, you will also see a neat javascript-and-CSS navigation menu. Good stuff. I also invite you to admire the simple, minimalist design that I am arrogantly and, most importantly, irrationally proud of.

You can view the code for this site on GitHub here, or inspect it with your browser, if you'd like. Also, feel free to read the nonsense I have prepared for you in the form of placeholder text below.
